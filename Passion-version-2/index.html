TEAM DARK

Hello Shadow fans

This is my second version of the passion project. For this iteration, I've decided to focus on another set of main characters from the Sonic franchise, which was Team Dark. Shadow is actually my favorite character of all the games, so I wanted to make a version of the website focusing on him and his allies.

I've changed the layout of this website a bit to better match the tone of Shadow's character and his role as a sort of anti-hero in the games.

Reasons why I like Shadow the Hedgehog

  • Cool character design
  • Interesting backstory and powers
  • The way he fits into the larger story for Sonic
