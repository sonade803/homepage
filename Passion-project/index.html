TEAM SONIC!!!



Welcome Friends!

I decided to do my passion project on my love for Sonic the Hedgehog and the various types of media that came out of it. I grew up watching people play the games, listening to the soundtracks for the games, and watched the various animated spin-offs for the franchise. For my first version of the website, I would like to focus on Team Sonic, which consist of some of the original main characters of the games.
